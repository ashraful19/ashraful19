## 👨‍💻 About Me

I am a systems-focused Full-Stack Engineer with 10+ years of experience architecting, scaling, and maintaining production SaaS platforms. My core philosophy balances clean domain-driven design with aggressive pragmatism-shipping high-availability software that drives measurable business growth.

* 🚀 **SaaS Track Record:** Architected core multi-tenant engines, custom commission logic, and global payout systems for high-traffic platforms including **Partnero**, **MyZenTeam**, and **Gymwol**.
* 🤖 **Shipped AI Systems:** Built **Partnero AI** (an autonomous program builder featured on Product Hunt) and authored **Laravel AI Orbit** (an observability dashboard for tracing AI agents).
* ⚡ **AI-Augmented Velocity:** Deeply integrate Agentic Workflows, RAG, and terminal-based sub-agents (Claude Code, Cursor, Codex, OpenCode) into my day-to-day engineering loop to maximize output.
* 🥋 **Execution Duality:** Equally comfortable acting as a solo architect taking a product from zero-to-one, or leading, reviewing, and mentoring distributed engineering teams.
* 🎓 **Academic Foundation:** Hold an **M.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering** and a **B.Sc. in Computer Science**.
* 📍 **Global Availability:** Based in Dhaka, Bangladesh • Seamless async & real-time collaboration overlap across **US (EST/PST)** and **European (CET)** business hours.
